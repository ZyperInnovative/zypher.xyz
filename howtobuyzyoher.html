How to Buy Zypher Points

Step-by-step Guide to Securely Purchasing Zypher Points

Follow These Steps

1. Open the Dashboard and Click "Buy Points"

The deposit address will be automatically copied to your clipboard.

2. Send Solana to the Deposit Address

You can send any amount of Solana to the address provided.

3. Copy the Transaction Hash/ID

Retrieve the transaction hash from your wallet, you can find it below in the transaction details or Solana Explorer.

4. Verify Your Transaction

Go back to the app and click "Verify Transaction" to proceed.

5. Enter the Transaction Hash

Click "Fetch Transaction" and wait for the confirmation.

6. Points Will Reflect in Your Dashboard

Check your transaction history and dashboard for updated points.

Why Use Transaction Hash?

We use transaction hashes to validate transactions for added security. Here’s why it’s crucial:

  • Preventing Fraud: By verifying the transaction hash, we ensure the deposit matches your account, preventing unauthorized access or manipulation.
  • Protecting Your Wallet: Unlike direct wallet authentication, using transaction hashes minimizes the risk of exposing sensitive wallet data to malicious actors.
  • Transparency: The transaction hash allows us to trace your deposit on the blockchain, ensuring accuracy and transparency in every step.
  • Speedy Validation: Fetching the transaction hash from the blockchain lets us confirm deposits in real-time without additional checks.

Tips for a Seamless Experience

  • Double-check the Deposit Address: Always ensure the copied deposit address matches the one displayed in the app before transferring funds.
  • Save Your Transaction Hash: After sending the funds, keep your transaction hash in a secure location for easy access during verification.
  • Use Reliable Wallets: Use trusted wallets like Phantom or Solflare for smooth and secure transactions.
  • Monitor Your Transaction: You can track your deposit status using Solana Explorer by pasting your transaction hash.
